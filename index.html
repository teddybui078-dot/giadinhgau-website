🍯 Our story

How it all began

It started, as the best things do, with a kitchen too small for everyone and a pot of soup that somehow always stretched to feed one more. Over the years the table got longer, the laughs got louder, and the family grew its own gravity.

We are not a tidy family. We are a warm one — quick to tease, quicker to forgive, and always five minutes late because someone had to find their other shoe.

This little site is where we keep the good stuff: the faces, the trips, the in-jokes that make no sense to anyone else. Welcome in.

📍 A favorite place

The cabin by Pine Lake

Two hours north, past the gas station with the good donuts, sits a little cabin that has hosted more family arguments about board games than anywhere on earth. We love it there. The water is cold, the wifi is worse, and nobody wants to leave.
