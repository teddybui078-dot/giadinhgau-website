The family

The whole, lovely bunch

Six personalities, one couch, infinite opinions on what to watch. Here they are — one at a time, before they all start talking at once.

← Back home

Hùng

Papa Bear

Master of weekend pancakes and the world's slowest barbecue. Hùng is the household's designated calm — the one who reads the instructions, labels the leftovers, and somehow always finds the missing shoe.

He measures barbecue in hours, not minutes, and counts a Saturday well spent if at least one neighbor wandered over for "just one plate."

Known for
Pancakes the size of dinner plates.
Never without
A spatula and an opinion about charcoal.
Catchphrase
"It's done when it's done."

Mai

Mama Bear

Keeps everyone warm, fed, and somehow always laughing. Mai is the reason the soup always stretches and the front door is never quite locked.

She can read a room, a fever, and a fridge with one glance, and she has never in her life let a guest leave hungry — or empty-handed.

Known for
Feeding people who "weren't even hungry."
Never without
A thermos and a plan B.
Catchphrase
"Sit, sit — I'll make you a plate."

Bảo

Big Brother

Lego architect, aspiring astronaut, undefeated at hide-and-seek. Bảo builds cities on the living-room floor and defends them with the seriousness of a city planner.

His current project has been "almost finished" for roughly two years, which everyone agrees is exactly how it should be.

Known for
A Lego skyline with its own zip code.
Never without
A half-built spaceship in one pocket.
Catchphrase
"I'm not done building yet."

Linh

Little Sister

Collects stickers, hugs, and oddly specific facts about whales. Linh is the family's smallest and loudest source of joy, and the reigning champion of the dinner-table interruption.

She will, given the slightest opening, tell you how a blue whale's heart is the size of a small car. You will remember it forever.

Known for
The whale facts. So many whale facts.
Never without
A fresh sticker sheet.
Catchphrase
"Did you know…"

Bà Ngoại

Grandmother

Tells the best stories — and, lovingly, the longest ones. Bà Ngoại is the keeper of every recipe, every birthday, and every grudge she pretends to have let go of.

House rule, established by unanimous and slightly fearful agreement: you do not interrupt the story.

Known for
Stories with no discernible ending.
Never without
A cup of tea slowly going cold.
Catchphrase
"Now, where was I…"

Cookie

The Dog

Firmly believes every snack is a shared snack. Cookie wandered in one afternoon in 2016, sat down, and has been supervising the kitchen ever since.

Officially nobody's responsibility, and unofficially the most important member of the household.

Known for
A snack radar accurate to the millimeter.
Never without
Hopeful, unwavering eyes.
Catchphrase
(an enthusiastic tail wag)

That's everybody.

Now come see what this lot gets up to when you point a camera at them.
